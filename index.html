About

Hi! I'm Steven.

I'm a full-stack developer who builds web and fun games.

When I was a kid, I was really into playing with LEGO bricks. I believe the time I spent turning my imaginary design into actual figures sparked my interest in building complex creations using simple pieces and exploring the possibilities of new technologies. As a developer, I aspire to create thoughtful and fruitful digital worlds for people.

Currently, I am working at Keywords Studios, a leading video game services company in Montreal. Before that, from 2016 to 2019, I studied at McGill University and completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Economics.

I'm also a dancer, a board game enthusiast, and a world citizen at heart.
